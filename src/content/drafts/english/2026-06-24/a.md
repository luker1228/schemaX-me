## Promote vs. Improve
The Collocation Trap: Promote vs. Improve
在中文里，我们常说“提升能力”，所以很容易直译成 "promote"。但在英文中：

Promote 通常指“职位晋升”（如：He was promoted to manager）或“产品宣传/推销”（如：promote a product）。

Improve / Enhance / Upgrade 才是用来表达“提升、改进”某种技能。

另外，表达“沟通能力”时，复数名词短语 "communication skills" 比单独一个不加修饰的 "communication" 要地道得多。


After several unsuccessful interviews, he resolves to improve his communication skills.
